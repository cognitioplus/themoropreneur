Privacy Policy

Your trust is essential. We are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012.

Information We Collect

We collect personal data only for legitimate purposes: names, contact details, demographic information, and program participation records from scholars, partners, staff, and community beneficiaries—primarily through forms, interviews, surveys, and digital platforms.

Why We Use Your Data

Your information supports program delivery (e.g., scholarships, MHPSS, training), monitoring & evaluation, donor reporting, safeguarding, and communication. We never sell or rent your data to third parties.

Data Security & Confidentiality

TMI implements physical, technical, and organizational safeguards—including secure storage, access controls, and staff training—to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Your Data Privacy Rights

Under RA 10173, you have the right to: access your data, correct inaccuracies, request deletion (where applicable), and withdraw consent. Submit requests to our Data Protection Officer via email.

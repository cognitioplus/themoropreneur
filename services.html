ANGAT DAPAT Portfolio

Innovative people-led solutions to optimize resources and promote social cohesion, economic empowerment, and peace in Bangsamoro and conflict-affected communities.

MHPSS Support

Mental Health and Psychosocial Support (MHPSS) integrated into peacebuilding, normalization, and post-crisis recovery for ex-combatants, GBV survivors, and conflict-affected families.

Digitalization

Bridging the technology gap through digital literacy training, e-commerce enablement, and tech-integrated social enterprises for marginalized Bangsamoro youth and women.

Climate & Circular Economy

Community-based climate adaptation, waste-to-value initiatives, and sustainable agriculture models that build resilience and reduce environmental vulnerability.

Halal Economy & Tourism

Development of halal-compliant products, cultural tourism routes, and Bangsamoro culinary branding—co-created with local entrepreneurs and aligned with Islamic values.

Peacebuilding & P/CVE

Preventing and countering violent extremism through interfaith dialogue, youth engagement, community peace spaces, and reintegration support for former combatants.

Research & Policy

Evidence-based studies, gender analyses, and policy drafting for national and international partners—including UNDP, EU, WFP, and Bangsamoro ministries.

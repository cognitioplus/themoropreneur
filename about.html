Our Mission &
Framework

Established in 2015 and registered with the SEC (CN 201616423), The Moropreneur, Inc. (TMI) envisions “self-sufficient, empowered, disaster-ready, peaceful, and sustainable Bangsamoro communities.” We co-create this future by empowering marginalized individuals, families, and groups through innovative, people-led solutions that bridge gaps in communication, relationships, technology, and policy.

Our Integrated Programs

ANGAT DAPAT

A holistic framework integrating 21 thematic areas—from climate adaptation and halal economy to peacebuilding and digitalization—designed to uplift conflict-affected and disaster-prone communities.

UPGRADE

Capacity-building program focused on leadership, organizational development, strategic planning, and values transformation—equipping individuals and institutions for effective, sustainable impact.

HARP

Humanitarian and Resilience-building Program delivering rapid response during emergencies—food aid, MHPSS, livelihood support, and incident command systems in crisis zones like Marawi.

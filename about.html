About The Moropreneur, Inc.

Co-creating self-sufficient, peaceful, and sustainable Bangsamoro communities since 2015.

Our Vision

“Self-sufficient, empowered, disaster-ready, peaceful, and sustainable Bangsamoro communities.”

Our Mission

To co-create self-sufficient communities by empowering marginalized individuals through innovative, people-led solutions that bridge gaps in communication, relationships, technology, and policy.

Core Programs

ANGAT DAPAT

A holistic framework integrating 21 thematic areas—from climate action and halal economy to peacebuilding and digitalization.

UPGRADE

Capacity-building for individuals and organizations in leadership, strategic planning, and values transformation.

HARP

Humanitarian and Resilience-building Program delivering rapid response during emergencies—food, MHPSS, and livelihood support.

Verified Low-Risk Organization

Validated by external audits from UN Women, UNDP, and Islamic Relief Philippines.

Regional Presence

Satellite offices in Basilan, Sulu, Tawi-Tawi, Marawi, Maguindanao, Zamboanga, Iligan City, and Cotabato City (Head Office).

Key Milestones

  • Trained 20,000+ scholars in entrepreneurship across BARMM
  • Co-authored UNDP & EU-commissioned research on Mindanao’s informal economy and gender
  • Technical writer for the Bangsamoro Education Code
  • Launched the first Mindanao Halal Food Culture Experience with DOT
  • Supported normalization efforts for former MILF combatants
  • Co-established CDA-registered women- and ex-combatant-led cooperatives
  • Facilitated 63 youth-led SDG projects with UNDP, UNFPA, and ILO
  • Provided humanitarian aid during the Marawi siege
